How do I get there?

Arriving by plane

The closest airport to Évora is Lisbon Airport, which is served by all major airlines.
Other options are Faro Airport (Algarve) or Porto Airport.

Getting from Lisbon to Évora


Car:

The fastest way is by rental car. The distance is about 133km, which takes around 1h 20min.
Please note that depending on the route, you'll have to pay toll charges for both the bridge and the highway.
Both can be paid directly on route at a toll house.
Parking is available at the Hotel.

Train:

From the airport you take the subway Vermelha - S. Sebastião and get out at the stop Oriente.
It takes 6 minutes and the subway leaves around every 5 Minutes.
From Lisbon-Oriente the train leaves to Évora 4 times a day, at 07:02, 09:02, 17:02 and 19:02, taking 1hr 33 min.
The schedule might change, so there will be an update a month before the wedding.

Tickets can be booked here: https://www.cp.pt/passageiros/en/buy-tickets

Bus:

The Bus to Lisbon Sete Rios leaves hourly from Lisbon Airport Arrival Terminal and takes about 25min.
When you have arrived at Lisbon Sete Rios, you have to walk for about 500m.
You take the Bus to Évora which leaves around hourly and takes between 1hr 30min and 2hr to get you to Évora,
depending on what time you take the bus.

The best bus company we found so far is Rede Expressos. You can buy tickets at:
https://www.rede-expressos.pt/en/tickets

At the moment there are also 2 busses a day from Lisboa Oriente to Évora, at 15:00 and 16:00.
The bus from there to Évora takes about 1hr 30min.
For info on getting to Lisboa Oriente from the airport, check the Train section above.

The schedule might change, so there will be an update a month before the wedding.
