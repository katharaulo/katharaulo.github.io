The place

The wedding will take place at the M'ar De Ar Aqueduto in the city of Évora, Portugal.

Rooms and Rates

Since we have an agreement with the hotel for exclusivity, we were able to get the rooms for special rates.
So there's no need for you to make any bookings at the hotel, all bookings will be organized through us.

The rates are
290 € for a double standard room
340 € for a superior double room (terrace/balcony)

All rates are for three nights and include a breakfast each day.
Please understand that we only can offer the rates per room and not per person.
